About Us :

Department of Information Technology, Yeshwantrao Chavan College of Engineering (YCCE), Nagpur has organized Two Days National Level Technical Fest “ITECHROOTS 10.0” during 23rd - 24th August 2019. The programme was approved by Computer Society of India (CSI), New Delhi.

The convener of the Technical Fest was Prof. Rajesh C Dharmik, Head of Information Technology Department. The Chief Guest of the program was Dr. S.V.C. Kameswara Rao , General Manager ISRO RRSC, Nagpur . Dr. Sachin P. Untawale, CEO , Meghe Group of Institutions, Nagpur YCCE was the President of the program.

The Technical Fest was coordinated by Prof. Amol D Gaikwad. The various events conducted in “iTECHROOTS 10.0”, were “Photoarena”, “Minute to win it”, “Code Battle”, “Treasure Hunt”, “Web Coding”, “OK Google”, “Game zone”.

Two Workshop were also conducted on ”Ethical Hacking” and “AI & Machine Learning” Around 1000 students from various technical & non-technical institutions participated in this Technical Fest.

The programme was aimed to provide platform for the students to exhibit their talent in various technical and nontechnical events. The programme concluded with awarding prizes, mementos and certificates to the winners and participants of various events.
